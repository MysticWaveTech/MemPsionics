MemPsionics Practice

  • For shapeshift: Picture your body transforming into something new. See the transformation happen in your mind's eye.
  • For exnihilate a (object): Imagine an object in your surroundings changing its material. See it change in your imagination.
  • For teleport: Visualize yourself instantly moving to another place. Picture the new location in your mind.
  • For invincible: Feel a powerful energy shield surrounding and protecting you. Sense its strength as it forms in your mind.
  • For telekinesis: See an object move through your focus and intent. Visualize the object being moved without touching it.

Press on each image one by one and after you press the image imagine yourself doing the ability, repeat for each image.

Now practice without pressing use your brain muscles and imagine yourself doing the ability after finishing the image and repeat for each image.

Now do each one from memory without the images infront of you and that's starting psionics.
